## 🏰 The Rover and the Village (My Own Game) — Final Assessment

### Story

> *A wandering mage travels far from home and arrives at a quiet village. He befriends the villagers, earns their trust, and together they stand united against an incoming enemy threat.*

### Game Overview

A single-player **3D Tower Defense** game built in Unity. Players take on the role of the wandering mage, interact with villagers through a dialogue system, and defend the village from waves of enemies.

- **Genre:** 3D Tower Defense
- **Engine:** Unity 2022.3
- **Multiplayer:** Not supported (single-player only)

### Systems

- 🎒 **Inventory System** — Item management and pickup
- 💬 **Dialogue System** — NPC conversations with branching dialogue
- 🚩 **Event Flag System** — Trigger-based event and quest progression
- ⚔️ **Combat System** — Covers player, NPC allies, and enemy AI behavior
- 🎞️ **Animation System** — Animations for player, NPCs, and enemies
- 🎬 **Simple Animation System** — Lightweight animation utilities

### How to Open

1. Download or clone the `my game` folder.
2. Open **Unity Hub** (version 3.3.4 or a compatible recent version).
3. Click **"Add"** and select the `my game` folder.
4. Make sure you have **Unity 2022.3.x** installed via Unity Hub.
5. Open the project and hit Play!

> ⚠️ Other Unity Hub versions may also work, but 3.3.4 is confirmed compatible.
